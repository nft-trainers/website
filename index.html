TRAINERS

What are Trainers?

Trainers are randomly created, nostalgic NFTs that resemble sprites from the old Pokemon video games!

Not only are Trainers full of nostalgia and creativity, but they'll also pay homage to the original CryptoPunks NFT by remaining in their sprite forms as well as having some guest appearances from the Zombie, Ape and Alien variants!

Each Trainer is individually crafted to have a near endless number of combinations such as hair and clothing styles/colors, gender, poses, backgrounds, throwing balls and more to truly shake things up and be unique to each and every owner. Who knows, maybe you'll even spot some cultural icons along the way!

FAQ

How can I get started?

Get the Metamask chrome extension. Load it with ETH through services that allow you to change your money to ETH like Coinbase Pro. Finally, join our Discord server and head on over to our OpenSea page and start collecting!

Will prices ever change?

Our community comes first and if there's ever a need to adjust the bonding curve, we will let everyone know in advance. That being said, the current bonding curve is extremely generous!

When do drops happen?

Drops will be announced on our Twitter page or Discord server in advance. We will also have some Trainers drop randomly so whoever happens to be checking out our page will be rewarded.

What kind of events are in store?

These are still being meticulously thought out and planned but some ideas include airdropping NFTs that enter you into lotteries, surprise drops with extremely rare Trainers, trivia nights and more! We're open to suggestions as well.

Is this a good investment?

With any NFT, there is always the risk of your investment not growing. We're hoping that we can engage with our community and create master quality Trainers so that they'll continue to grow more and more valuable. As always, use your best judgment and don't spend what you don't have!
